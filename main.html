FAQ'S

What am I signing up for?
You are signing up to our mailing list that will provide updates on our progress, let you know when the Launch starts, and when/where you can download the Groupii app.
Are you serious; will my account be free forever?
Yes, your account will remain free forever unless specified otherwise.
I am going on multiple trips will people be able to see all of my trips?
No, only the trips you share publicly will be visible to others.
When is all payments required for the trip?
Payment deadlines will be communicated to you in advance via email.
Do I have to share my location with the group?
Sharing your location is optional and can be managed in your settings.
Host trips are public, are the hosts able to decide who goes on the trip?
Yes, hosts have full control over who can join their trips.
